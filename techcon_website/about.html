Our Journey: The History of TechCon

Since its founding in 2010, TechCon has grown from a regional developer meetup into a globally recognized conference. Each year, we bring together thousands of tech professionals to share knowledge, shape innovation, and celebrate the evolution of technology.

Our Mission

TechCon exists to spark ideas, foster collaboration, and accelerate technological progress. We aim to empower developers, entrepreneurs, and innovators to shape a more connected, inclusive, and forward-thinking digital future.
